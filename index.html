Get to Know Us
A grassroots sadaqah jariyah project bridging the gap in Northern Nigeria so no Muslim is left without the Book of Allah. Every donation turns into a lasting spiritual impact.

Between July 2025 and March 2026, the initiative has distributed over 120,000 Qur'ans across three major cities in Northern Nigeria — Kano, Zaria, and Jos — reaching mosques, madrasas, prisons, and underserved rural communities.

Testimonial

What our Client Say?

From Riyadh to Kuwait City, donors trusted us with their Sadaqah Jariyah — and we delivered. Every Qur'an is tracked, photographed, and confirmed. Here is what they said when the Holy Book reached Northern Nigeria.

A

I donated 50 Qur'ans in memory of my late mother. Within three weeks, Naba Assiraj sent me photos and a video of the distribution at a masjid in Maiduguri. Seeing children reciting from those copies — I wept. I have donated again for my father.

Abdullah Al-Qahtani
Riyadh, Saudi Arabia 🇸🇦 · 50 Qur'ans
F

I was skeptical at first — you never know which organisations are genuine. But Naba Assiraj sent a delivery report with photos from Kano State less than a month after my transfer. One photo showed a young boy holding the very Qur'an I donated. We all donated together the second time.

Fatima Rashid Al-Mansoori
Dubai, UAE 🇦🇪 · Family donation · 120 Qur'ans
K

My company sponsored 200 Qur'ans for our Ramadan CSR initiative. Naba Assiraj coordinated everything professionally — we received the imam's name, the village, and a signed receipt from the community leader in Sokoto. Our team was moved. We will renew this every Ramadan, insha'Allah.

Khalid Nasser Al-Sabah
Kuwait City, Kuwait 🇰🇼 · Corporate · 200 Qur'ans
M

After donating, I asked exactly where my Qur'ans went. The team called me, explained the distribution area in Zamfara, showed me photos of the school, and followed up two months later to confirm pupils were using them in daily classes. SubhanAllah — this is transparent charity.

Mariam Hassan Al-Zayani
Manama, Bahrain 🇧🇭 · 75 Qur'ans · Zamfara State
S

I donated 30 Qur'ans in my children's names. Naba Assiraj printed a note in each copy with their names as donors. When my daughter saw the photo, she made du'a and asked to save her own pocket money for more. This charity teaches children the value of giving.

Saleh Ibrahim Al-Ghamdi
Jeddah, Saudi Arabia 🇸🇦 · 30 Qur'ans · Children's names
H

A group of sisters in Abu Dhabi heard about Naba Assiraj and we pooled 100 Qur'ans in one week. The Qur'ans arrived in Maiduguri, were stamped with our donation details, and handed to a Tahfiz school. We received a group photo and a video. Alhamdulillah, it felt like we were there.

Hessa Butti Al-Muhairi
Abu Dhabi, UAE 🇦🇪 · 100 Qur'ans · Maiduguri Tahfiz school
